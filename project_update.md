PRD: Stone Coating & Resinous Website

1. Project Overview

   Build a corporate, premium, professional website for Stone Coating & Resinous, a flooring renovation company serving Oklahoma.

   The website should feel established, serious, high-end, and trustworthy. It should not look like a generic AI-generated website. No emojis, no overly playful design, no cheap-looking graphics, and no generic stock-heavy layout.

   The website should follow a structure similar to Stronghold Construction but adapted for a flooring renovation company.

2. Primary Website Goal

   The main goal is to generate qualified project inquiries through a professional online presence.

   Primary objectives:

   Capture quote requests through a contact form.
   Build trust with homeowners, commercial clients, contractors, property managers, and industrial clients.
   Explain the company’s flooring renovation services clearly.
   Showcase projects once photos and videos are provided.
   Position Stone Coating & Resinous as a premium, corporate, professional flooring renovation provider in Oklahoma.

6. Design Direction
   Visual Style

   The website should look corporate and professional, similar in seriousness to Stronghold Construction.

   Use:

   Dark charcoal / black backgrounds
   Warm neutral tones
   Stone gray
   Concrete texture accents
   Metallic bronze, gold, or muted copper accent color
   White space
   Large professional typography
   Clean grid layouts
   Strong photography
   Minimal but premium animations

   Avoid:

   Emojis
   Cartoon icons
   Generic AI-looking gradients
   Overly colorful sections
   Cheap construction clipart
   Overloaded text blocks
   Unprofessional stock images
   Suggested Color Palette

   Primary:

   Charcoal / near black: #111111
   Deep gray: #2B2B2B
   Concrete gray: #8A8A8A
   Off-white: #F5F3EF

   Accent:

   Muted gold / bronze: #B68A4C
   Warm stone beige: #D8C7A3
   Typography

   Use professional fonts such as:

   Headings: Montserrat, Oswald, Playfair Display, or Inter Tight

   Body: Inter Regular
